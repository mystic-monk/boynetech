Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device. They're widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, or gather usage statistics. "Similar technologies" includes things like local storage, which behaves the same way for the purposes of this policy.

2. Do we use cookies?

No. This website does not set tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies, and we don't use third-party scripts that do either. We deliberately built the site this way so you don't need to manage cookie preferences here. There's nothing to opt out of.

3. What we do collect instead

Our web server keeps standard access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and diagnostic purposes only. This is not stored via cookies and is not used to track you across sites or sessions. Our Privacy Policy covers this in more detail.

4. Links to other sites

Pages on this site may link to third-party sites (for example, LinkedIn). Those sites have their own cookie practices, which we don't control. Check their policies if you're concerned.

5. If this changes

If we ever add cookies, for analytics, embedded video, or similar, we'll update this page first to name the cookie, its purpose, how long it lasts, and how to decline it. We won't add tracking cookies silently.

6. Managing cookies in your browser

Even though we don't set any, you can control or clear cookies for any site at any time through your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge all support this from their privacy/settings menu).

7. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@boynetech.ie.
