Why Join Us

What working here is actually like

We'll be honest with you, because that's what we'd want in your position.

Small team, real ownership

You won't be one of 400 consultants assigned to a workstream. You'll own things, with support, accountability, and credit. When something ships, your name is on it.

Varied, meaningful work

Analytics, AI, infrastructure, and software, across public sector and enterprise. No two engagements are the same. If you want depth and variety without changing employer every two years, this is the place.

Flexibility and respect

We judge by outcomes, not hours. Remote-first, with regular team collaboration days in Dublin. We trust people to manage their own time, and we back that up with how we actually operate.

We don't have open roles right now

We're a small, deliberately senior team, so we hire infrequently, but we keep a shortlist of strong candidates for when the right opportunity comes up. If that sounds like you, send us your CV.
