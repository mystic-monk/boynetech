Our Work

The Problems We're Built to Solve

We're actively building our first client engagements. Here's where we focus and how we approach these kinds of challenges.

We haven't published client case studies yet. We're deliberately getting our first engagements underway before we do. What we can share is the kind of work the practice is built around, based on the experience and expertise the team brings.

Example engagement types

Public Sector

Replacing fragmented reporting with a modern, governed analytics platform

Many public sector organisations are running critical reporting off a patchwork of Excel files and manual extracts. We design and build proper data platforms — medallion architecture, governed Power BI dashboards, single source of truth — so teams stop fighting their data and start using it. This is work we know inside out, and it's one of the clearest wins available to Irish public bodies right now.

AI & Data

Making large document libraries searchable with AI, entirely on your own infrastructure

Organisations with large policy libraries, procedural documents, or knowledge bases spend enormous amounts of time on manual search. We build AI-assisted retrieval systems — fully on-premise, no data leaving the network — that meet the governance requirements of public bodies and regulated industries. The technology is mature; most organisations just haven't had someone build it for them properly yet.

Enterprise

Building an analytics function from scratch when the data is there but the capability isn't

Some organisations have decent data but no structured way to use it. We do end-to-end capability builds: tooling selection, governance frameworks, initial reporting layer, and where needed, support with hiring and onboarding the right people. The goal is a function that keeps running after we leave, not a dependency on us.
