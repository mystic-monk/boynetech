Public Sector

Replacing a legacy reporting stack with a modern analytics platform across a national transport authority.

A government transport body needed to retire a fragmented collection of Excel-based reports and manual data extracts. EireTech designed and delivered a medallion-architecture data platform on Microsoft Fabric with governed Power BI dashboards.

  • Reporting cycle reduced from 5 days to same-day
  • Single source of truth for operational and planning data
  • Self-service analytics adopted by 40+ staff
