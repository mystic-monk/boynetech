Example Engagements

The problems we're built to solve

We haven't published client case studies yet — we're deliberately getting our first engagements underway before we do. What we can share is the kind of work the practice is built around, based on the experience and expertise the team brings.

Public Sector

Replacing fragmented reporting with a modern, governed analytics platform

Many public sector organisations are running critical reporting off a patchwork of Excel files and manual extracts. We design and build proper data platforms: medallion architecture, governed Power BI dashboards, a single source of truth. Teams stop fighting their data and start using it. This is work we know inside out, and it's one of the clearest wins available to Irish public bodies right now.

AI & Data

Making large document libraries searchable with AI, entirely on your own infrastructure

Organisations with large policy libraries, procedural documents, or knowledge bases spend enormous amounts of time on manual search. We build AI-assisted retrieval systems that run fully on-premise, with no data leaving the network, and meet the governance requirements of public bodies and regulated industries. The technology is mature; most organisations just haven't had someone build it for them properly yet.

Enterprise

Building an analytics function from scratch when the data is there but the capability isn't

Some organisations have decent data but no structured way to use it. We do end-to-end capability builds: tooling selection, governance frameworks, initial reporting layer, and where needed, support with hiring and onboarding the right people. The goal is a function that keeps running after we leave, not a dependency on us.

AI & QA

Synthetic test data and AI-generated test coverage for software teams

Testing against production data usually means privacy and compliance risk, and hand-writing test cases doesn't scale with delivery speed. We build synthetic data generators that produce realistic, non-sensitive datasets for test and staging environments, and use AI to generate and maintain test case coverage as an application evolves. Safer environments, and QA that keeps pace with the codebase instead of trailing behind it.

Custom Build

Every organisation is different.

We build from scratch, scoped to what you actually need: data engineering, AI, operational tooling, and process automation, always grounded in your context rather than what looks impressive on a proposal.

Being one of our first clients means senior attention on every part of the engagement, not just kickoff. We're selective about what we take on, and we're not trying to scale fast at the expense of doing good work.
